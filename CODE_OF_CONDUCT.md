# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors, maintainers, and members of this community pledge to make participation in the **LLM Security Toolkit** project a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

This applies regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We are committed to building a community where people feel safe to contribute, ask questions, share ideas, and disagree constructively because that is how good open-source software gets built.

---

## Our Standards

### ✅ Behaviour we encourage

- Using welcoming, inclusive, and patient language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the project
- Showing empathy toward other contributors, everyone starts somewhere
- Crediting others for their ideas and work
- Asking questions openly there are no stupid questions here

### ❌ Behaviour that will not be tolerated

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks of any kind
- Discriminatory jokes or language based on identity, background, or ability
- Posting or threatening to post someone's private information ("doxxing")
- Deliberate trolling, inflammatory comments, or sustained disruption
- Sexual or violent language, imagery, or unwanted advances
- Dismissing or belittling contributions based on experience level
- Continued contact with someone after they have asked you to stop

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces, including:

- GitHub Issues, Pull Requests, Discussions, and code review comments
- The project's documentation and README
- Any communication channel (Discord, email, social media) where you are representing this project

It also applies when an individual is representing the project in public spaces — for example, at a conference, meetup, or online event.

---

## Enforcement

### Reporting

If you witness or experience behaviour that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it by emailing the project maintainer directly:

**Vladlen — [contact via GitHub @vladlen-codes]**

All reports will be handled with discretion and confidentiality. You will receive an acknowledgement within **72 hours** and a resolution update within **7 days**.

When reporting, please include:
- A description of the incident
- Where and when it occurred
- Any relevant links, screenshots, or context
- Whether you would like to remain anonymous

### What happens after a report

Every report will be reviewed by the maintainer. Depending on severity, the response may include:

| Severity | Example | Action |
|---|---|---|
| **Minor** | Unconstructive comment, dismissive tone | Private warning + request to edit or remove |
| **Moderate** | Repeated rudeness, targeted criticism | Formal warning + temporary restriction from participation |
| **Severe** | Harassment, threats, doxxing | Permanent ban from all project spaces |

All enforcement decisions are final. If you believe a decision was made in error, you may appeal by replying to the resolution email with additional context.
